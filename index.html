About Me

The Designer
Behind the Work

I'm Balaguru K, a creative designer based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, with a passion for transforming ideas into compelling visual narratives.

Currently pursuing my B.E. in Computer Science & Engineering at Latha Mathavan Engineering College, I blend technical precision with artistic vision. My design journey began with a curiosity for how brands communicate, and has evolved into a deep expertise in visual identity systems.

I've had the privilege of interning at VVR Studios and MAINCRAFT Technologies, where I honed my skills in branding, social media design, and print materials. Every project is an opportunity to push creative boundaries while staying true to the brand's essence.
